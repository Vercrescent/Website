About Us

Vercrescent Clothing was founded in January 2017 by highschool students Rohan Kalra and Hamzah Ahmed. Together they set out to start a brand that would not only provide fashionable clothing but inspire and help young entrepreneurs and students explore their passions. Students who want to pursue fashion design and digital marketing have been given that opportunity by Vercrescent. Everything that Vercrescent produces, physically or virtually, is done by students. Providing students with unique opportunities and high-quality clothing is what the Vercrescent brand is all about.

Upcoming

In the coming years, we will be working to further expand our reach. Vercrescent was started as a simple clothing line, but we will push to include other aspects of pop culture such as music, podcasts, and other media outlets. Vercrescent Clothing is also going to make an even stronger initiative to ensure that students are given access to opportunities provided by Vercrescent. While doing this, Vercrescent will continue to abide by the pledge towards high quality clothing and inspiration for young entrepreneurs. But for now, stay tuned for new clothing coming soon in late 2022
